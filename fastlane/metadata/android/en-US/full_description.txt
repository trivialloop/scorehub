ScoreHub is a simple and intuitive application for tracking game scores across multiple players.

Features:
• Multi-game support
• Player management with custom colors
• Comprehensive statistics and rankings
• Top 20 high scores leaderboard
• Win/loss/draw tracking
• Multi-language support (English and French)
• Fully offline

Perfect for board game enthusiasts who want to keep detailed records of their gaming sessions!

Currently supported games:
- Yahtzee
